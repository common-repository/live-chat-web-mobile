Add Chat Inside your wordpress website and answer instantly from your mobile

== Description == 
You can add chat inside your wordpress website and then collect instantly the messages sent from your website on your mobile and answer directly from your mobile.
This is a fantastic tool to manage real time all your customers messages.
Very useful also for dating, psychic, support, Marketplace websites ........
